FRICTION BROWSER EXTENSION - PRIVACY POLICY

Last Updated: December 30, 2024

OVERVIEW
Friction is a browser extension designed to help users break mindless browsing habits. This privacy policy explains how the extension handles user data.

DATA COLLECTION
Friction does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All data is stored locally on your device.

LOCAL DATA STORAGE
The extension stores the following data locally in your browser using Chrome's local storage API:
- List of websites you choose to block
- Timestamps of when each site was added to your blocked list
- Daily visit counts for each blocked site
- Per-tab state to track friction completion

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to the extension developer or any third parties.

NO TRACKING
Friction does not:
- Track your browsing history
- Collect personal information
- Use analytics or tracking tools
- Send data to external servers
- Share data with third parties

DATA CONTROL
You have full control over your data:
- All data can be cleared by removing the extension
- Individual sites can be removed from your blocked list (after a 14-day commitment period)
- No account or login is required
